My favarite animes

One Piece

The anime of my life is One Piece. It's a story about Monkey D. Luffy, who wants to become the King of the Pirates. His story is really engaging, filled with numerous adventures and a plethora of cool characters. The story spans more than 100 volumes and has yet to reach its conclusion.

Hunter x Hunter

Another anime that I like is Hunter x Hunter the story is about Gon, who wants to find his father and understand the reason of his father leaving him alone.
